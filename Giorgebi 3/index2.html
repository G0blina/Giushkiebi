Bate's Hotel
Hotel City is a boutique hotel located in the heart of Tbilisi. Latest renovation was made in 2021. The Art Deco interiors of the hotel feature a harmonious combination of soft colors and modern accessories that create an atmosphere of welcoming symbolic of Tbilisi. Just a 3 minute walk to the iconic Freedom Square and Rustaveli Avenue, guests find themselves immersed in the rich culture of Georgia with its museums, theaters, and galleries. As they step out of our doors, the beauty of Georgian heritage is revealed to them on the picturesque streets of Old Tbilisi with its ancient churches, family-owned shops and stunning architecture. On nearby Shardeni street, a famous locale, unforgettable evenings await with numerous cafes, restaurants and bars offering the best of Georgian cuisine and, of course, wine. The unique design of the hotel creates a comfortable environment for guests to relax after a busy day and high speed wireless internet gives guests the confidence to work remotely. Hotel City offers shuttle service as well as guided tour to all of the regions of Georgia for surcharge. Whether for business or pleasure - Hotel City offers the best of Tbilisi. This is our guests' favorite part of Tbilisi, according to independent reviews. Couples in particular like the location - they rated it 9.9 for a two-person trip.
